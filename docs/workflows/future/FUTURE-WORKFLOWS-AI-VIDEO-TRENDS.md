## WF-46: Cinematic Flythrough (Dynamic Camera Movement)

**Status**: ✅ Complete Specification
**Launch Target**: Month 3-4
**Documentation**: `/SwiftList/WF-46-CINEMATIC-FLYTHROUGH-SPECIFICATION.md`

### What It Does
Creates cinematic "flythrough" videos with dynamic camera movement through AI-generated scenes. Camera zooms, pans, flies through environments with speed ramping + living elements (rain, cars, people walking).

### Why This is #2 Priority
- ✅ **Highest viral potential** ("AI is the new camera crew")
- ✅ **Content creator demand** (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram creators need this)
- ✅ **95% cheaper than drone footage** ($7.50 vs $500+)
- ✅ **Impossible shots** (underwater, space, haunted mansions, cyberpunk cities)

### The 4-Step Process

**Step 1: Design Concept**
Choose cinematic theme (Urban Melancholy, Cyberpunk, Mountain, Desert, Underwater, etc.)

**Step 2: Generate 5 Keyframe Images (Nano Banana Pro)**
Each image = camera position in flythrough sequence
```
Scene 1: [description], [camera position], [environment], [mood]
Scene 2: [description], [camera position], [environment], [mood]
...
Scene 5: [description], [camera position], [environment], [mood]
```

**Step 3: Generate Transitions (Kling AI 2.5 Turbo)**
For each keyframe pair (1→2, 2→3, 3→4, 4→5):
```
Seamless cinematic transition from scene [N] to scene [N+1],
camera movement: [smooth forward motion / zoom / pan / fly],
[environment details: rain, traffic, pedestrians, etc.],
add living elements: people walking, cars driving, rain falling,
cinematic camera work, smooth motion, professional film quality,
maintain mood: [contemplative, dramatic, etc.]
```

**Step 4: Speed Ramping + Sound Design**
Apply speed curves (fast → slow → fast) + AI-generated sound (rain, music, SFX)

### 8 Cinematic Themes

1. **Urban Melancholy** - Rainy city streets, neon reflections, lonely figures
2. **Neon Cyberpunk** - Futuristic city, holographic ads, flying vehicles
3. **Sunrise Mountain Pass** - Mountain roads, golden hour, misty valleys
4. **Desert Wasteland** - Abandoned structures, dust storms, harsh sun
5. **Underwater Reef** - Coral reefs, sunlight rays, marine life
6. **Haunted Mansion** - Gothic architecture, flickering candles, shadows
7. **Tokyo Night Life** - Busy crosswalks, vertical neon, trains
8. **Arctic Expedition** - Ice fields, northern lights, research stations

### Target Markets
- **Content Creators**: YouTube intros, TikTok transitions, Instagram Reels
- **Real Estate Marketers**: Luxury property cinematic tours
- **Music Artists**: Music video B-roll, album art animations
- **Game Developers**: Concept art for environment design
- **Film Students**: Pre-visualization for film projects

### Pricing & Economics
- **Cost**: $2.64 (Claude $0.015 + Nano Banana $0.125 + Kling $2.40 + ElevenLabs $0.10)
- **Price**: 175 credits ($8.75 with sound design)
- **Margin**: 70%
- **Duration**: 20-25 seconds, 60fps, 1920×1080

### Revenue Projection (Year 1)
```
Month 6: 120 users × 1.5 videos/month = 180 videos
Revenue: $1,485/month
Profit: $1,017/month (68.5% margin)
Year 1 Cumulative: ~$12,000 profit
```

## RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### Risk 1: Video Quality Inconsistency
**Mitigation**:
- Implement quality scoring pre-delivery
- Auto-reject outputs below threshold (re-generate with fallback model)
- Offer "Re-generate" button (free, limited to 2× per job)

### Risk 2: High COGS
**Mitigation**:
- Start with higher credit pricing (60-80 credits vs 40 for static)
- Monitor margins monthly, adjust pricing as needed
- Negotiate volume discounts with providers

### Risk 3: API Downtime
**Mitigation**:
- 3-tier fallback system (Runway → Pika → Luma)
- Queue system: retry failed jobs after 5 minutes
- Transparent status page showing provider health

### Risk 4: User Expectations Mismatch
**Mitigation**:
- Show example videos for each workflow
- "Preview" feature (low-res quick generate before paying)
- Clear descriptions of what each workflow does/doesn't do
