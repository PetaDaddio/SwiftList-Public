### ENGRAVING (4 presets)

Perfect for: Finance, Corporate, Heritage, Premium Brands

#### 1. Steven Noble Black & White ⭐
- **Style**: Museum-quality steel engraving
- **Technique**: 100% black ink on white paper, cross-hatching
- **Best For**: Finance, Logos, Currency, Certificates, Formal
- **Color Palette**: Pure B&W (#000000, #FFFFFF)
- **Reference**: `/CMO/.../Steven Noble.../Black + White and 1-color/`

#### 2. Steven Noble Hand-Tinted Color ⭐
- **Style**: 19th century chromolithography
- **Technique**: Black engraving + vintage color washes underneath
- **Best For**: Packaging, Labels, Heritage Brands, Botanical
- **Color Palettes**: Vintage (madder lake, prussian blue, terre verte)
- **Reference**: `/CMO/.../Steven Noble.../Color Illustrations/`

#### 3. Lyle Hehn Black & White ⭐
- **Style**: Bold relief print (linocut/woodcut)
- **Technique**: Hand-carved aesthetic, carved typography
- **Best For**: Signage, Brewery Art, Folk Art, Festival Posters
- **Color Palette**: Charcoal & Oatmeal (#1a1a1a, #f5f5dc)
- **Reference**: `/CMO/.../Lyle Hehn/`

#### 4. Lyle Hehn Color (McMenamins) ⭐
- **Style**: Surreal-historical psychedelic illustration
- **Technique**: Bold linocut outlines + thick acrylic fills
- **Best For**: Brewery, Historical Whimsy, Mystical Commercial

- **Reference**: `/CMO/.../Lyle Hehn/`

## Usage Instructions

### 1. Browse Presets
Visit SwiftList preset marketplace and explore by:
- Category (Engraving, Illustration, Print, Digital, etc.)
- Use case (Finance, Crypto, Brewery, Travel, etc.)
- Featured presets (⭐ starred)

### 2. Preview on Your Product
Upload your product image and preview how each preset looks applied to your product before committing.

### 3. Select & Apply
Choose your preferred preset and submit job. No additional charge for official presets (included with workflow credits).

### 4. Choose Color Palette
Many presets offer multiple color palettes. Select the one that best matches your brand.
